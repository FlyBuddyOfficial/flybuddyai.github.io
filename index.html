What is Fly Buddy?

Fly Buddy is your AI-powered aviation copilot. From emergency support to smart planning, it is built to assist pilots in real-time with critical decisions, proactive alerts, and voice interaction. Think of Fly Buddy as a second brain in the cockpit, helping reduce stress, improve safety, and optimize every flight.

Fly Buddy integrates real-world data like weather, NOTAMs, aircraft profiles, and AI-generated logic to guide pilots with step-by-step instructions and conversational support. Whether you're handling a bird strike, managing fuel burn, or navigating turbulence, Fly Buddy has your back.

Key Features

Voice-Activated Emergency Help

Say “Mayday” and Fly Buddy immediately activates step-by-step voice guidance, helping manage airborne crises calmly and efficiently.

Live Weather & NOTAM Integration

Real-time updates on METARs, TAFs, and NOTAMs, directly in the cockpit to enhance awareness, route safety, and efficiency.

AI-Enhanced Decision Support

Fly Buddy recommends safer alternates, better flight paths, and fuel-saving tweaks using advanced aviation-trained AI to reduce pilot workload and improve operational safety.

Pilot Stress Reduction System

A calm, conversational AI copilot designed to keep you composed, alert, and efficient in any situation — like a second brain beside you in the cockpit.
